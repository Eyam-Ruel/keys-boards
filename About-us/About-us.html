About Us

Connecting Generations Through Music

Welcome to Keys & Boards - Keys & Boards

Keys & Boards is an intergenerational web platform designed to connect music lovers from Gen Z to Seniors. Our mission is to create a vibrant community where people can share musical skills, life experiences, and audio stories across age boundaries.

Whether you're a vinyl collector, a digital music producer, a jazz enthusiast, or simply someone who loves to share stories about music, Keys & Boards provides the space for meaningful connections.

Our Mission

To bridge generational gaps through the universal language of music, fostering knowledge exchange, cultural appreciation, and lasting friendships between people of all ages.

Core Values

Inclusivity

We welcome music lovers of all ages, backgrounds, and skill levels. Everyone has something valuable to share and learn.

Accessibility

Our platform is designed with high contrast, readable fonts, intuitive navigation, and multilingual support to ensure everyone can participate comfortably.

Respect & Safety

We maintain a safe, respectful environment where all members can share their passion for music without fear of harassment or discrimination.

Knowledge Sharing

From vinyl restoration techniques to modern music production, we facilitate the exchange of skills and experiences across generations.

What We Offer

  • Community boards organized by music genres and topics
  • Direct messaging for one-on-one conversations
  • Calendar for scheduling music sessions and meetups
  • Map-based exploration to find local music enthusiasts
  • Audio and video sharing capabilities
  • Hashtag system for easy content discovery

Academic Background

Keys & Boards was developed as part of the International Open Bidding Project, a collaborative academic initiative involving multiple international institutions.

Partner Institutions

IUT de Bobigny, Université Sorbonne Paris-Nord (France)
University of Tirana (Albania)
Hanoi University of Science, VNU (Vietnam)

This project represents a commitment to creating technology that serves social good and promotes intergenerational understanding through shared cultural experiences.
