Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Keys & Boards

Getting Started

What is Keys & Boards?

Keys & Boards is an intergenerational platform that connects music lovers of all ages. Whether you're interested in jazz, vinyl collecting, music production, or any other musical topic, Keys & Boards helps you find and connect with like-minded people across generations.

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Get Started" button on the homepage or navigate to the Sign Up page. Fill in your information including your name, email, location, and musical interests. You'll need to accept our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy before creating your account.

Is Keys & Boards free to use?

Yes, Keys & Boards is completely free to use. All features including messaging, community boards, calendar, and map exploration are available at no cost.

Using the Platform

What are Music Boards?

Music Boards are community spaces organized by genre or topic (like #Jazz, #MAO, #Vinyl). Here you can share posts, ask questions, share audio/video content, and engage in discussions with other members who share your interests.

How do I connect with other members?

You can explore members through the Explore page, which includes a map view to find people near you. Click on any profile to view their interests and skills, then use the "Connect" button to send them a connection request. Once connected, you can message them directly.

Can I share audio and video content?

Yes! You can share audio recordings, video clips, and images in your posts on Music Boards. This makes it easy to share performances, tutorials, or your music collection with the community.

What is the Calendar feature?

The Calendar (Agenda) feature allows you to schedule and manage music sessions, meetups, and events with your connections. You can create events, invite members, and keep track of upcoming musical activities.

Privacy & Safety

Is my personal information safe?

Yes. We comply with GDPR regulations and take your privacy seriously. Your email address is not shared with other members unless you choose to do so. All data is stored securely and you have full control over your information. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

What if I encounter inappropriate behavior?

We have a strict code of conduct. If you encounter harassment, hate speech, or any inappropriate content, please report it immediately through the internal messaging system or contact us at azad.oztopal.pro@gmail.com. We take all reports seriously and will take appropriate action.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. Go to your profile settings and select "Delete Account", or contact us directly for assistance.

Technical Support

Which browsers are supported?

Keys & Boards works best on modern browsers including Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version for the best experience.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, Keys & Boards is a web-based platform optimized for both desktop and mobile browsers. You can access it from any device with an internet connection. A dedicated mobile app may be developed in the future.

What languages are available?

Keys & Boards currently supports English, French, and Spanish. You can change the language from the settings menu at any time. We plan to add more languages in the future.
